### about me

i make things that probably shouldn't exist yet. creative technologist at **NYU ITP** working at the intersection of AI, physical computing, and interactive experiences. currently hungry (always hungry).

i believe the most interesting things happen when you smash together disciplines that don't usually talk to each other — like speech recognition and fashion, or eye-tracking and HTML tag.

<br>
